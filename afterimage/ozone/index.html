Ozone (2025)

Ozone is an anti-game created for the 2025 Global Game over the course of 3 days. Presenting itself as a regular incremental game such as Cookie Clicker, it aims to criticise capitalist greed by subverting the expectation of infinite resources that are commonly assumed in similar games.

Objective: Accumulate as many bubbles (BUPS) as possible.

The game offers four types of placeable buildings:

  • Factory - Generates BUPs, but slowly siphons volume from the atmosphere
  • Core Extractor - Generates many more BUPs, but instead siphons volume from within the planet
  • Forest - Restores the volume of the atmosphere, but costs BUPs to maintain
  • Core Pump - Pumps volume from the atmosphere into the planet. Also costs BUPs to maintain.

At a first glance, the player might assume that in order to progress further, they would have to maintain a balance between the 4 buildings. However, the balancing is intentionally skewed and the game ends in one of two states:

  • Ending one: The player accumulates mass wealth, but pops the planet in the process. A congratulations screen is shown as they have succeeded in their objective.
  • Ending two: The player only uses the first two building to build up an initial fund, then only places down the restorative buildings. Due to their maintenance costs, the player is no longer able to exponentially accumulate wealth. The game goes on forever, with no reward or praise.

Essentially, the only way to not destroy the bubble planet is to reject the initial objective. The player is left to draw their own metaphors.
